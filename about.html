Bio

As a seasoned product leader, I have a special inclination on building products that are user-friendly and technically strong. I love building products and have successfully bootstrapped 3 startups in the Nigerian ecosystem. Understanding user behavior is my passion, and I strive to create optimized experiences that ease pain points and bring happiness. My approach is data-driven, using analytics to gain insights and make informed decisions that lead to growth.

My experience in web engineering has given me a strong foundation in problem-solving and attention to detail, which has been instrumental in working collaboratively with cross-functional team members to develop requirements, visualizations, models, and prototypes. In addition to my technical expertise, I have ample experience in data analytics, internet marketing, and conversion optimization. I use these skills to identify market opportunities, evaluate risks and trade-offs, and prioritize initiatives that align with the company's strategic goals.

I have also had the opportunity to speak on career programs and inspire product managers, helping them to build awareness and develop the skills and knowledge they need to succeed. This has allowed me to hone my leadership skills and gain a deeper understanding of the product development process.

By thinking through the entire customer journey, I am able to challenge assumptions and identify opportunities to improve the product experience. As a product leader, I am committed to using data and analytics to drive innovation and deliver products that exceed our customers' expectations.

 
 
 
 
 
 
 

Noteworthy Skills

I bring a unique blend of skills to the table. I have a deep understanding of the complete product lifecycle, from customer interviews and journey maps to hypothesis validation and data analysis. I am also well-versed in digital marketing, design, and UX principles, which allows me to create products that not only meet the needs of users but also exceed their expectations.

Throughout my career, I have worked with various tools and technologies, including Notion, Google Sheets, MixPanel, Mode, SalesForce, Figma, Whimiscal, Optimizely, and more. I am skilled in using data to gain insights into user behavior and make informed decisions that drive growth.

In addition, I am experienced in using various project management tools such as Linear, Jira, Asana, and Clickup, and I am familiar with SCRUM, Agile, and Linear methodologies. I am also proficient in using SQL, RegEx, Python, Pandas, PHP, JSON, APIs, and HTML/CSS. I’ve used LangChain, and OpenAI for projects.

Overall, my experience and skills allow me to approach product management holistically, combining technical expertise with a deep understanding of user needs and market dynamics.
